History

We are a group of three recent accounting major graduates. Who decided to venture out together in the field of accounting and created this firm recently. We specializes in tax accounting and business consulting related to small businesses, Audit specialists and Legal business consultants.

Values

We value it most of all to provide excellent services that contribute to our clients’ accomplishment of their goals. We do so by providing high-quality, cost-effective solutions for our clients in a professional, client-friendly and efficient manner.

Our communication style is proactive and responsive. We recommend effective solutions after listening to our clients’ concerns and goals, and researching the facts. We strive for excellence, integrity and accuracy in the quality of our work.

Corporate Responsibility

At KPG Accounting, we are focused and committed to make a difference in the area of corporate responsibility. As part of this fundamental obligation, we are committed to the appropriate protection and use of personal information of our clients by any means possible.

At the same time, we also believe in giving back to the society. And, for that we try to arrange/participate in different social service events together with some other firms time to time.

Ethics

We at the KPJ, strictly adheres to the ethics values of our profession. We are completely straightforward and honest in all the professional deals that we do with our clients. We do not allow bias, conflict of interest or undue influence of others to override our business judgments. We definitely respect respect the confidentiality of information acquired as a result of professional and business relationships and we do not not disclose any such information to third parties without proper and specific authority unless there is a legal or professional right or duty to disclose. We believe that confidential information acquired as a result of professional and business relationships should not be used for the personal advantage of the professional accountant or third parties. We also comply with relevant laws and regulations during our business transactions with our clients
